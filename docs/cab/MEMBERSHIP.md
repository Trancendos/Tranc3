## Onboarding New Members

1. New member reviews `CHARTER.md` and `APPROVAL_WORKFLOW.md`.
2. Chair adds member to the Forgejo `cab-reviewers` team.
3. New member observes two approval cycles before voting independently.
4. Update this register with the new member's role and handle.
