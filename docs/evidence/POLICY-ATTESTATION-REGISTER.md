## 4. Process

1. Compliance owner distributes policy documents via PROC-TRN-001
2. Staff read and confirm understanding via attestation form
3. Signed attestation stored in this register
4. Non-completion escalated to line manager after 30 days
5. Annual attestation renewal required by 30 September each year

## 5. Privileged Access Attestation

Personnel with admin/privileged access must additionally attest to:
- Understanding of CAB change management process
- Responsibility for maintaining audit trail integrity
- Prohibition on circumventing security controls
